Online Resources

  • Course Website

    • Course information, lecture notes, tutorial material, important announcements, etc. will be posted on the course website. It is your responsibility to visit it frequently.

  • Beginner Tutorial for MySQL
  • Email Communication

    • Questions regarding the course material, assignments, midterm, etc. should be posed on the Piazza board. Questions about the assignment which you feel you cannot formulate without revealing your solution (should be very rare!), as well as questions and concerns regarding your personal matters should be directed to the instructor.

  • Discussion Board

    • You are encouraged to use the Piazza board to discuss the course material, pose questions on the assignments, etc. The Piazza board will be monitored by the instructor and by your TAs. Please, be very careful not to reveal (parts of) your solution on the Piazza board.

  • MarkUs

    • You will be using the MarkUs platform to form groups, as well as submit assignments for this course. You can sign in to MarkUs using your uoft credentials.

      3. ssh utorid@mathlab.utsc.utoronto.ca.
